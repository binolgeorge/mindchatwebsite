COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MindChat products, ordering, and support.

Do I need to purchase an EEG headset to use MindChat?

Yes, to get the full benefits of the MindChat platform, you'll need an EEG headset. We recommend the Muse 2 headset, which is fully compatible with our platform and priced at $249. The headset is required to capture brain activity data for mental health monitoring and concussion detection.

How do the different subscription plans differ?

Our Enterprise Solution ($37.50/user/month) is designed for clinics, schools, sports teams, and corporations needing comprehensive multi-user management. The Specialist Referral Directory ($39/provider/month) is for healthcare providers wanting to be listed in our directory. The Consumer App Subscription ($3.99/month) is for individual users seeking personal mental health monitoring.

Can I try MindChat before subscribing?

For our Enterprise Solution, we offer demonstrations to qualified organizations. Please use the "Schedule a Demo" button to arrange a personalized walkthrough of our platform. For individual users interested in the Consumer App, you can download the app and explore its basic features before subscribing to the full service.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscription management is easy with MindChat. For Enterprise and Specialist Referral Directory clients, contact your account manager or our support team to modify or cancel your subscription. Consumer App users can manage their subscription directly through the app settings or their app store account.
