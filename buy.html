COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MindChat products, ordering, and support.

Do I need to purchase an EEG headset to use MindChat?

Yes, an EEG headset is needed for brain activity insights. We recommend the Muse 2 ($249, sold separately), compatible with our platform for capturing data relevant to mental wellness monitoring and concussion awareness support.

How do the different subscription plans differ?

We offer distinct solutions:

  • Enterprise Solution ($37.50/user/month, billed annually): Ideal for organizations (clinics, schools, teams, companies) needing multi-user dashboards, customizable protocols for mental wellness & concussion awareness monitoring, and administrative oversight.
  • Specialist Referral Directory ($39/provider/month): A B2B listing service for qualified healthcare providers to increase visibility and manage referrals via the MindChat network.
  • Consumer App ($3.99/month): Designed for individual users (patients, students, employees) for personal mental wellness tracking and insights, integrating with compatible EEG headsets.

Can I try MindChat before subscribing?

Yes! For organizations interested in the Enterprise Solution, please use the "Schedule a Demo" button to arrange a personalized walkthrough tailored to your needs.

For the Consumer App, you can download it and explore basic features before deciding to purchase the monthly subscription for full access to monitoring and insights.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Managing your subscription is straightforward:

  • Enterprise & Specialist Directory Clients: Please contact your dedicated account manager or reach out to our main support team via the Contact Page to discuss modifications or cancellation.
  • Consumer App Users: You can manage your monthly subscription directly within the app's settings section or through your respective app store account (Apple App Store or Google Play Store).
