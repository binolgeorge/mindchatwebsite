Our Story

About MindChat

Our mission is to enhance mental wellness and concussion awareness using evidence-based technology, making brain health insights more accessible.

OUR MISSION

Revolutionizing Mental Health Monitoring

To enhance mental wellness and concussion awareness monitoring using AI-guided assessments and EEG feedback, providing timely, objective insights to supplement traditional approaches.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make AI+EEG mental health and concussion monitoring a new standard of care globally, offering objective, timely insights built on validated clinical tools and EEG biomarkers.

Our Story

MindChat was born from observing the limitations of traditional mental health and concussion protocols, which often rely heavily on subjective reporting.

We saw a critical need for accessible, evidence-based technology to provide objective data, address missed diagnoses, reduce delays in care, and empower individuals, providers, and organizations with actionable brain health insights.

Our Approach

We combine:

  • AI-Guided Assessments: Using validated protocols (like PHQ-9, BAI) via conversational AI.
  • EEG Integration: Leveraging compatible devices (e.g., Muse 2) to capture brain activity correlates related to mental states and potential concussion impact markers.
  • Objective Insights: Enabling early awareness, potential for personalized plans, and continuous monitoring to supplement subjective reports.

MindChat aims to make objective data a standard part of monitoring brain health for providers, schools, teams, and corporations globally.
