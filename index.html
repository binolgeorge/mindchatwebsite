Critical Problems We're Solving

MindChat addresses three major crises in health and wellness with our revolutionary AI and EEG platform.

Concussion Detection Crisis

69M+1
people sustain traumatic brain injuries annually
>50%2
of concussions go undetected due to unreliable self-reporting
  • Standard tests may miss cases leading to persistent symptoms (approx. 30%3).
  • Impacts without direct head trauma are often missed visually.
  • Delayed awareness can hinder proper management & recovery.

Sources: 1 Maas et al., 2017 Lancet Neurol. 2 UPMC Sports Med. 3 Based on CDC Concussion Guideline data, 2023.

Mental Health Crisis

1 in 5
adults experience a mental-health disorder each year4
~25%5
of adolescents reported elevated depression symptoms during 2020 lockdowns
  • High treatment costs ($500–$2,500+/month) limit access6.
  • Severe shortage of providers creates long wait times.
  • Current solutions often rely solely on self-reporting.

Sources: 4 WHO Fact Sheet, 2022 (using 12-month prevalence). 5 Racine et al., 2021 JAMA Pediatrics. 6 Healthline cost survey, 2023.
